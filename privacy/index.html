Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mall. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When creating an account, we may collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, and other necessary information to operate the app.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions within the app, including pages visited, items viewed, time spent, and features used. This helps us improve app performance and user experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-related data such as your model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and network information.
  • Location Data (Optional): If enabled, we may collect location data to personalize content, improve recommendations, or support app features. You can enable/disable location permissions at any time from your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: We use your information to operate and improve Mall, including account management, customer support, and essential app functionality.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: Usage and location data help us tailor products, offers, and content to your preferences.
  • To Communicate: We may send updates, notifications, or promotional content. You may opt-out of promotional messages at any time.
  • For Analytics: We analyze usage patterns and behaviors to enhance app performance and improve the overall experience.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your information. We may share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners that help us operate the app (e.g., analytics, cloud hosting, payment gateways).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or company restructuring, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Data Security

We apply appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Although we strive to safeguard all information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Edit: You may review and update your personal information through your account settings.
  • Delete Account: You may request account deletion and removal of your data by contacting our support team.
  • Permissions: You may manage location, notifications, and other app permissions directly from your device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

The Mall app may use third-party tools or services such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase Services
  • Crash Reporting Tools
  • Payment Processing Services

These providers may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Adjustments will be posted here with an updated effective date. Continued use of Mall after updates means acceptance of the new policy.
